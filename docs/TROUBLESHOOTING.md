# Troubleshooting Guide

## Common Issues

### Deposit Fails

**Error: ERR_BELOW_MINIMUM**
- Solution: Deposit at least 1 STX

**Error: ERR_ALREADY_DEPOSITED**
- Solution: Withdraw existing deposit first

### Withdrawal Fails

**Error: ERR_LOCK_ACTIVE**
- Solution: Wait until lock period expires

**Error: ERR_WITHDRAWAL_COOLDOWN**
- Solution: Wait 1 day between withdrawals

### Badge Issues

**Badge not minting**
- Check reputation >= 1000 points
- Ensure badges contract is authorized

## Getting Help
