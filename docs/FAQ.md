# FAQ

## General Questions

### What is BitSave?
BitSave is a decentralized savings vault on Stacks where you can lock STX and earn reputation points.

### How do I earn badges?
Badges are automatically minted when you reach 1000+ reputation points.

### Can I withdraw early?
Yes, but you'll pay a 20% penalty and earn no reputation points.

## Technical Questions

### What is the minimum deposit?
1 STX (1,000,000 microSTX)

### What is the reward rate?
Currently 10% annual rate, adjustable by admin.

### How long can I lock my STX?
From 1 day to ~10 years (144 to 525,600 blocks)

## Security Questions

### Is my STX safe?
Yes, the contract has reentrancy protection, input validation, and overflow protection.

### Who controls the contract?
The admin can adjust parameters but cannot access user funds.
